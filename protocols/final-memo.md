# Protocol: Final Memo

The final memo is decision-grade synthesis.

## Required sections

1. Executive summary
2. Decision context
3. What is known
4. What is uncertain
5. Taxonomy / landscape map
6. Key patterns
7. Anti-patterns
8. Opportunity map
9. Risk map
10. Recommended experiments
11. Decisions / implications
12. Next research questions
13. Evidence notes
14. Decision log entry

## Rule

Do not hide uncertainty. Do not over-optimize for polish at the expense of traceability.
