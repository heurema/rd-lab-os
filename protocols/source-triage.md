# Protocol: Source Triage

## Source modes

- academic
- official_docs
- code_repo
- benchmark
- product_docs
- market_news
- social_signal
- expert_blog
- internal_doc
- unknown

## Trust levels

- A: primary source, official docs, paper, code, benchmark, dataset
- B: expert secondary analysis with clear sourcing
- C: news/reporting, market commentary, social signal
- D: low-quality, stale, SEO, unsourced

## Rule

Social signal sources can support claims about attention, sentiment, or market chatter. They should not support scientific or technical truth claims by themselves.
