# Protocol: Critic Checklist

The critic is adversarial but constructive.

## Checklist

- Are any major claims unsupported by ledger rows?
- Are citations actually supporting the claims they are attached to?
- Are primary sources missing?
- Are old sources used for current claims?
- Are social signals overused as truth evidence?
- Are counterarguments missing?
- Are recommendations too strong for the evidence?
- Is the report actionable?
- Could another worker reproduce the artifact chain?

## Output format

```text
Critical issues:
Medium issues:
Minor issues:
Missing searches:
Claims to remove or weaken:
Sources to verify:
Final confidence rating:
```
